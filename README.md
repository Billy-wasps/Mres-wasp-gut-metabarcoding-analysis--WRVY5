This is all the data analysis done by WYVR5 in the completion of "What’s the buzz? Analysis of Aculeate and parasitoid wasp diet using DNA metabarcoding" Mres project 1 supervised by Seirian Sumner, Iona Cunningham-Eurich, and Gavin Broad. 
AFFILIATIONS: UCL, Centre for Biodiversity and Environment Research
MRes Biodiversity, Evolution, and Conservation, 4th year of study
BIOS0013: MRes Biodiversity, Evolution & Conservation Research Project I 25/26

This is the analysis of metabarcoding data of B.juncea guts and includes: NMDS, composition analysis, read count comparison, seeing effect of density on diet composition diversity, bipartite network generation, species accumulation curves and analysis of spread of predation  behaviour.
